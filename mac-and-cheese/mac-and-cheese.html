Mac and Cheese

Description

The classic macaroni and cheese made using my home recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of macaroni (200-300g)
  • 500-600ml of milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • Mozzarella cheese and an additional cheese of your choice (Swiss, Parmesan, or Cheddar work well)
  • Optional vegetables (diced) and/or chicken (cooked, cut into bite-sized chunks)
  • Salt, black pepper, and red chili flakes (as needed)

Preparation

  1. Pour the milk into a large pot with the water and put the uncooked macaroni in.
  2. Add about 3-4 tablespoons of salt (and optional veggies and chicken) and begin cooking at medium-high heat.
  3. As the milk begins to thicken, stir lightly but constantly to prevent the milk from burning and the pasta from sticking to the bottom.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked and the milk reaches a creamy, sauce like consistency, reduce the heat to low.
  5. Add the rest of the seasonings and a portion of your cheese. Once it melts and mixes in, continue adding and stirring the same way until all of it is incorporated.
  6. Turn off the heat, ladle out the pasta into a serving tray or plate and enjoy!
